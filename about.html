Ambica Pharma - Since 2005

Ambica Pharma has evolved from a humble startup into a leading force in the pharmaceutical industry. With over 12 years of expertise, we pride ourselves on innovation, quality, and client satisfaction.

As a professionally managed company, we focus on continuous growth, delivering pharmaceutical products that meet global standards while maintaining ethical practices and total quality management.

Headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, Ambica Pharma holds ISO-9001-2015 certification and follows stringent WHO guidelines for production. Our GMP-certified unit ensures quality at every step, from formulation to delivery.

With over 1000+ formulations—ranging from tablets and capsules to syrups, injections, and cosmetics—we ensure the highest quality, timely delivery, and competitive pricing to exceed customer expectations.


Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To revolutionize healthcare by delivering high-quality, affordable, and innovative pharmaceutical products that enrich lives and improve global well-being. We are driven by a passion for excellence, a commitment to sustainability, and a vision to create a healthier future for all.

Vision

To become a global beacon of pharmaceutical innovation and quality, setting the gold standard for excellence. We aspire to make advanced healthcare accessible to every corner of the world, empowering communities and creating a lasting legacy of trust and impact.
