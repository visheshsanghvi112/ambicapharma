Company History

Founded in 2005, Ambica Pharma has grown from a small startup to a renowned name in the pharmaceutical industry, known for its commitment to excellence and innovation. This is to introduce ourselves as a professionally managed fast-growing Pharmaceutical Company having more than twelve years of manufacturing and marketing experience. We are based in Mumbai (Maharashtra), The Company has been accredited with ISO-9001-2015 and manufactures pharmaceutical formulations as per the guidelines of WHO. It is a (GMP Certified) Unit with total quality management and In-house Testing Laboratory. Our success is the result of focusing on our clients' top priorities such as quality products, on-time delivery, competitive rates, and unparalleled responsiveness. Johnlee Pharmaceuticals manufactures more than 1000+ Pharmaceutical Formulation Products in the form of Tablets, Capsules, Dry syrups, Liquid Orals, Injections, Eye/Ear Drops, Cosmetics, and Sustained Release Preparations.

Quality Assurance

At Ambica Pharma, quality assurance is at the core of everything we do. We employ rigorous quality control measures to ensure that every product meets and exceeds industry standards. Our commitment to quality is supported by state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and a team of dedicated professionals who bring years of expertise to the table.

We continuously invest in technology and training to maintain our high standards and ensure customer satisfaction. Our quality assurance processes cover every aspect of production, from sourcing raw materials to final product testing, ensuring consistency and reliability in every batch.
